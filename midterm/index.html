The Life of a Starfish

A young girl was walking along a beach upon which thousands of starfish had been washed up during a terrible storm. When she came to each starfish, she would pick it up, and throw it back into the ocean. People watched her with amusement. She had been doing this for some time when a man approached her and said, “Little girl, why are you doing this? Look at this beach! You can't save all these starfish. Starfish have existed on Earth for at least 450 million years, and are what is known as a keystone species in their habitat. A keystone species is a species that has a large effect on the ecology and natural environment in which it lives. Starfish move across the ocean floor using small tube feet which cover their underside.Starfish are among the most popular and recognizable types of marine life, which is due partly to their appealing shape, and also due to the fact that they live in many ocean habitats, from rock pools and shallows to coral reefs and the deepest depths. They are among the most well-known types of invertebrates.
